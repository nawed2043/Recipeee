Watermelon Brain-Freeze

⏱ Time: 5 Mins 🔥 Difficulty: Very Easy

Ingredients

  • 4 Cups seedless watermelon chunks
  • Juice of 2 Limes
  • 1 tbsp Sea salt (for the rim)
  • Crushed ice
  • Chef Altamash's secret: A pinch of chili powder

Chef Altamash's Instructions

  1. Throw the watermelon and lime juice into a blender.
  2. Blend until it looks like a pink sunset.
  3. Rub a lime wedge on the rim of your glass and dip it in salt/chili powder.
  4. Pour the mix over crushed ice.
  5. Drink it fast, but watch out for the brain freeze—I warned you!
