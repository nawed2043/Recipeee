Chef's Spicy Summer Tacos

"If you eat these standing up, the calories don't count. That's a Chef Altamash fact."

Prep Time: 25 Mins Difficulty: Medium Category: Savoury

Ingredients

  • 8 Corn tortillas
  • 500g Grilled chicken or Paneer cubes
  • 1 Cup Mango Salsa (Mango, onion, lime)
  • Chef Altamash's Secret Chili Mayo
  • Fresh Cilantro for garnish

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Warm your tortillas on a dry skillet until they get those beautiful little brown spots.
  2. Toss your protein in lime juice and chili powder, then sear until crispy.
  3. Place the protein in the center of the tortilla. Don't overfill it unless you want to wear your dinner.
  4. Top with a generous spoonful of mango salsa.
  5. Drizzle the secret chili mayo and finish with cilantro.
