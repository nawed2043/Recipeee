Grilled Peach & Halloumi Salad

"Yes, we are grilling fruit. Trust me, I'm a professional food-maker."

Prep Time: 15 Mins Difficulty: Easy Category: Savoury/Veg

Ingredients

  • 3 Fresh Peaches, sliced
  • 200g Halloumi cheese
  • Arugula (Rocket) leaves
  • Honey and Balsamic Glaze

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Lightly oil your grill pan. Grill the peach slices for 2 mins each side.
  2. Grill the Halloumi slices until they have golden grill marks.
  3. Lay a bed of arugula on a bright yellow plate.
  4. Top with peaches and cheese. Drizzle with honey and balsamic.
