Zesty Garlic Shrimp

"Shrimp are just tiny sea-warriors waiting to be dipped in butter. Don't keep them waiting."

Prep Time: 15 Mins Difficulty: Easy Category: Savoury

Ingredients

  • 1lb Large shrimp (peeled)
  • 4 Cloves of garlic (or 10, I don't judge)
  • Fresh Lemon juice
  • Red pepper flakes
  • Butter and Olive Oil

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Heat oil and butter in a pan until it starts to shimmer like the summer sun.
  2. Add garlic and red pepper flakes. Sauté for 1 minute until your kitchen smells like heaven.
  3. Add shrimp. Cook until they turn pink and curl into a 'C' shape. 'C' stands for 'Chef Altamash'.
  4. Squeeze half a lemon over the pan.
  5. Serve immediately with crusty bread.
