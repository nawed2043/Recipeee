Liquid Gold Mango Lemonade

⏱ Time: 10 Mins 🔥 Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 2 Ripe mangoes (pureed)
  • 4 Fresh lemons
  • 3 tbsp Honey
  • 1 Liter Sparkling water
  • Fresh mint & Ice

Chef Altamash's Instructions

  1. Puree the mangoes until they are smoother than my pick-up lines.
  2. Squeeze the lemons into a large pitcher.
  3. Mix the mango puree and honey with the lemon juice.
  4. Top with sparkling water and stir gently.
  5. Add ice and a mint leaf. If it looks fancy, you did it right!
